HOW TO PLAY: Drag a new ring from outside into the table. If a cell already has a ring or rings, you can add the new ring if it is larger than the existing ring or rings. You can re-drag the largest ring from one cell into another. If a cell contains all four sizes of rings, they all disappear and the cell becomes empty again.
